Pagoda

Details about Visitors Known for: Learning, Meditation, Architecture, Photography, and Travel. Entry Fee: There is no cost to enter. Additionally, there is no fee to attend a course; however, you must submit an online application. Hours of Operation: 6:30 a.m. to 6:30 p.m. Visit Length: The length of a visit is arbitrary. It could take an hour if you are taking a tour of the facility. Depending on how long the course is, attending one could take three or seven days. The Global Vipassana Pagoda is one of the distinctive sights in Mumbai. This monument, which honors the teachings of Lord Gautama Buddha, welcomes thousands of tourists every year. The Global Vippassana Pagoda is revered as a symbol of harmony and a haven of serenity.
